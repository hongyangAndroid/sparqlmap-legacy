Welcome to SparqlMap

SparqlMap is a RDB2RDF mapping tool that allows you to expose your data as:

  • SPAQL enpoints and
  • Linked Data.

R2RML

Is the spec for mapping relational databases into RDF. With SparqlMap you can use it to map CSV as well.

SPARQL

We support SPARQL 1.0 and a subset of SPARQL 1.1.
